# Privacy Policy for WebPayback Protocol

**Effective Date**: January 19, 2025  
**Last Updated**: January 19, 2025

## Introduction

WebPayback Protocol ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our platform, website, and services.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
When you register as a creator or interact with our platform, we may collect:
- Name and contact information
- Email address
- Social media profile information (when you verify platform ownership)
- Wallet addresses for blockchain transactions
- Website URLs and domain information

### Usage Information
We automatically collect:
- IP addresses and device information
- Browser type and version
- Pages visited and time spent on our platform
- Blockchain transaction data (publicly available on-chain)
- AI access patterns to your content (for reward distribution)

### Platform Verification Data
During verification process, we collect:
- Meta tags from your verified websites
- Platform-specific verification tokens
- Domain ownership verification data
- Content fingerprints for AI access detection

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Provide and improve our services
- Verify creator identity and platform ownership
- Distribute WPT token rewards for AI content usage
- Send updates, news, and information about WebPayback Protocol
- Ensure platform security and prevent fraud
- Comply with legal obligations
- Analyze platform usage and performance

## Information Sharing

### We DO NOT:
- Sell your personal information to third parties
- Share your data with advertisers
- Use your information for purposes outside this policy

### We MAY share information:
- With blockchain networks (wallet addresses for transactions)
- With platform verification services (Chainlink oracles)
- When required by law or legal process
- To protect rights, property, or safety
- With your explicit consent

## Data Security

We implement enterprise-grade security measures:
- Multi-layer fraud detection systems
- Reentrancy protection for smart contracts
- Encrypted data transmission and storage
- Regular security audits and monitoring
- Access controls and authentication systems

## Blockchain and Transparency

WebPayback Protocol operates on public blockchains. Please note:
- Wallet addresses and transactions are publicly visible
- Smart contract interactions are permanently recorded
- WPT token transfers are transparent and immutable
- Some platform data may be stored on decentralized systems

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal information
- Correct inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your data (subject to blockchain limitations)
- Opt-out of non-essential communications
- Withdraw consent for data processing
- Request data portability

## Data Retention

We retain your information:
- As long as your account is active
- As required for legal compliance
- For legitimate business purposes
- Until you request deletion (where technically possible)

Note: Blockchain data cannot be deleted due to the immutable nature of distributed ledgers.
