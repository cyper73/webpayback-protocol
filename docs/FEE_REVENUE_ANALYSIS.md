## Risk Assessment

### Revenue Risks
1. **Volume Doesn't Materialize**: Lower fees don't guarantee volume
   - Mitigation: Active marketing, partnerships, incentives
   - Backup: Can adjust fee rate if needed (though requires new contract)

2. **Competitive Pressure**: Other tokens also reduce fees
   - Mitigation: Focus on utility, not just fees
   - Advantage: First-mover benefit in creator reward space

3. **Market Downturn**: Crypto bear market reduces all volume
   - Mitigation: Diversified revenue streams beyond trading fees
   - Resilience: Lower fees make token more attractive in downturns

### Technical Risks
1. **Smart Contract Issues**: Bugs in V2 contract
   - Mitigation: Extensive testing, gradual rollout
   - Insurance: Maintain V1 as backup during transition

2. **Migration Problems**: Users struggle to migrate
   - Mitigation: User-friendly tools, clear instructions
   - Support: Dedicated help channels and documentation
